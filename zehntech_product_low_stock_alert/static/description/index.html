Multi-Level Alert Configuration

The form below is the Opportunity form, enhanced Enables low stock alerts to be configured at three levels: Product, Category, and Global. The system uses a priority-based method to determine which alert threshold to apply. This gives flexibility for managing different types of inventory.

Stock Alert in List View

This feature displays low stock warnings directly in the product list (tree) view. It visually highlights products that are below the defined alert quantity. This allows users to quickly spot inventory issues without opening individual product records. It improves efficiency in stock monitoring and helps take timely restocking actions.

Stock Alert in Kanban View

This feature displays low stock warnings directly in the product Kanban view. It visually highlights products that are below the defined alert quantity. This allows users to quickly spot inventory issues without opening individual product records. It improves efficiency in stock monitoring and helps take timely restocking actions.

Individual Product
Location : Select Individual Product -> Product -> Inside Product Variant

This feature allows you to set a custom low stock alert threshold for each product individually. It gives precise control over inventory management, especially for products with unique demand or critical stock levels. Users can configure alert quantities directly on the product form.

Product Category
Location : Select Category Product -> Congiguration -> Inside Product Categories

This feature enables you to define low stock alert thresholds at the product category level. It simplifies inventory management by applying a common alert quantity to all products within a specific category. Ideal for managing similar types of items, this approach ensures consistent monitoring and timely alerts across grouped products.

Alert Tag in POS (Point of Sale)
Location : Point Of Sale -> Continue Session

This feature displays a visual alert tag on low-stock products directly in the POS interface. It helps cashiers and store staff instantly identify items that need restocking, enhancing decision-making during sales. The alert ensures better inventory control and avoids overselling of out-of-stock products in real-time.

1. What is the Product Low Stock Alert module used for?

The Product Low Stock Alert module is used to monitor users when product quantities fall below a defined minimum threshold. It helps businesses prevent stockouts by providing alerts based on global settings, individual product configurations, or product category levels. It ensures timely restocking and smooth inventory management across Inventory and POS modules.

2. How Can I configure alert thresholds differently for each product?

Yes, the module supports multi-level configuration. You can define alert thresholds at the product level, or fall back to category-level or global settings if no specific value is set. The system automatically chooses the most specific configuration available.
